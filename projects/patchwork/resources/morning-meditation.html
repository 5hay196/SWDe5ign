üåÖ The 5-Minute Morning Practice

Find Your Space (30 seconds)

Choose a quiet, comfortable spot where you won't be disturbed. Sit comfortably with your back straight, either on a cushion, chair, or your bed. Turn off notifications on your devices.

Set Your Intention (30 seconds)

Close your eyes gently and take a moment to set a positive intention for your day. This could be something like "I choose peace," "I am open to possibilities," or "I approach today with kindness."

Deep Breathing (2 minutes)

Begin with three deep, cleansing breaths. Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts, then exhale through your mouth for 6 counts. Then return to natural breathing, simply observing each inhale and exhale.

Body Awareness (1.5 minutes)

Bring attention to your body. Starting from the top of your head, mentally scan down through your face, neck, shoulders, arms, chest, back, and legs. Notice any tension and consciously relax those areas.

Gratitude Reflection (30 seconds)

Think of three things you're grateful for right now. They can be simple: your comfortable bed, the morning light, or an upcoming opportunity. Feel the warmth of appreciation in your heart.

üí° Pro Tips for Success

  • Consistency over perfection: Even 2-3 minutes is better than skipping entirely
  • Use a gentle alarm: Set a soft, natural sound to end your session
  • Start small: If 5 minutes feels long, begin with 2-3 minutes and gradually increase
  • Same time, same place: Habits form faster with consistent timing and location
